Email Marketing

As an Email HTML Programmer working for Rocket Companies, I worked with design teams to bring beautiful marketing emails to life. Here's a small selection of emails I created!

Not familiar with email HTML? Well, it's not the same as web. For emails to render in Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, etc they must be coded using inline styling and structured using tables for layout. You're essentially working with code from the 90's - no Flexbox or Bootstrap here! This presents a unique challenge in bringing to life "modern" designs consumers expect to see. How is this valuable today? Let's put it this way - if you can build a house with nothing but a screwdriver and a hammer, imagine what you could do with power tools!
